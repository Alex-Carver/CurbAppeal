Who is Curb?

I grew up on the south suburbs of Chicago (Evergreen Park). Attended NIU for college where I studied Exercise Science. College is where I really fell into fitness. Originally I was a P.E. major with goals to be a gym teacher.

After my sophomore year I realized that wasn’t what I really wanted; I liked the aspect of teaching but maybe not necessarily in a classroom. After a few classes in the Exercise Science field I realized that was my calling. Soccer was also a big part of my life, I played for the travel team at NIU and began coaching at the high school level after I graduated.

Follow Me: @CurbAppeal

Why Train with Me?

When training with me, you should expect a very thorough and effective prescription of workouts that are tailored towards your goals. In addition to workouts, I also provide nutritional assistance as to how we can alter your diet so that it’s healthy and enjoyable.

I communicate regularly with my clients and treat them as friends, so you can also expect a “hey how’s your day going?”, it doesn’t always have to be fitness related. Lastly, you should expect results. My goal is to empower a better you, and with proper eating habits paired with superb training, you have a recipe for undeniable success.
